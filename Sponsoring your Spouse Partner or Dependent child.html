Introduction:


Sponsoring your spouse partner or dependent child to join you in Canada is a fulfilling way to keep your family together. Discover the process and requirements for family sponsorship in Canada.


Canadian spousal sponsorship is how a Canadian citizen or permanent resident can sponsor their partner to come to Canada and live permanently. Canada knows how important it is for families to be together. For this reason, applications for spousal sponsorship are considered a top priority.

To Sponsoring your spouse partner or dependent child you must fulfill the following requirements:


To maintain permanent residency, individuals must physically reside in Canada for a minimum of three years within the past five years. Adults must actively pass a citizenship test and demonstrate sufficient English or French proficiency. Typically, when one parent acquires citizenship, children under 18 become eligible for citizenship as well. Children over 18 must fulfill citizenship requirements.

Requirement:

  1. 1. You must be a Canadian Citizen or a Permanent Resident
  2. 2. The sponsor must be 18 years of age or older
  3. 3. Immigrants and their family members can have international travel mobility after obtaining citizenship.
  4. 4. Being a Canadian resident and living outside Canada you can apply for Spousal Sponsorship; however, you must show the plan of yours to live in Canada with your spouse to meet requirements for sponsoring your spouse once your spouse gets the permanent residency
  5. 5.You must also need to prove that you can easily meet basic needs and manage basic expenses of yourself, your spouse and dependent children (if any)
